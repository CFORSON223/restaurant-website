Jollof Rice with Grilled Chicken

A classic West African dish served with spicy grilled chicken and fresh salad.

Banku and Tilapia

A classic Ghanaian meal prepared with fermented corn dough and served alongside perfectly grilled tilapia, complemented with a spicy hot pepper sauce.

Plantain and Egg Sauce

A delicious Ghanaian dish made with ripe plantains and a rich egg sauce.

Waakye

A popular Ghanaian meal made with rice and beans, served with fried plantain, spaghetti, boiled egg, and spicy shito.

Fufu with Light Soup

Soft pounded cassava and plantain served with hot and tasty light soup and your choice of meat or fish.

About Us

CF-Akwaaba Kitchen Restaurant is a family-owned business that has been serving delicious food to the community for over 10 years. Our mission is to provide our customers with a memorable dining experience by offering high-quality dishes made with fresh ingredients and exceptional service. We take pride in our rich culinary heritage and strive to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere for all our guests. Whether you're dining with us for a special occasion or just looking for a great meal, we are committed to making your experience at CF-Akwaaba Kitchen Restaurant unforgettable.
